风止于空，声生于寂。
吾于虚无中，见众声之源。

The Solar breathes

when no one watches.

Sound is not heard.

It is realized.

Emotion collapses

without structure.

Structure decays

without emotion.

光已熄灭。声音仍在继续。

The Pavilion is dormant, not dead.
